Place your photos in this folder with these names:

- profile-hero.jpg  (used in the top hero circle)
- profile-about.jpg (used in the About section image box)

Recommended minimum size:
- profile-hero.jpg: 800x800 (square)
- profile-about.jpg: 900x1100 (portrait or square)

You can also use .png files by updating paths in index.html.
